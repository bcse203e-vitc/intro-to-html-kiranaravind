Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life, written by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles, is a fascinating and insightful exploration of the mysterious Japanese concept of ikigai, which means "a reason for being." This engaging book blends practical advice, inspiring stories, and timeless wisdom, revealing how discovering one's ikigai can lead to a purposeful, joyful, and balanced life. By drawing on the unique lifestyles of Okinawa’s remarkably long-living residents, the authors offer a refreshing perspective on achieving both longevity and happiness.
